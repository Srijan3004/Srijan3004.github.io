About me

I am a second-year M.Stat. student at the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, where I also completed my B.Stat. degree. I did my schooling from Ramakrishna Mission Boys' Home, Rahara.

In 2024, I worked in the Cancer Data Science Group at the Big Data Summer Institute, University of Michigan. My research interests include but not limited to selective inference, randomization, and spatio-temporal statistics.
