About Me

I'm a second-year M.Stat. student at the Indian Statistical Institute (ISI), Kolkata, where I also completed my B.Stat. degree in 2025. In the summer of 2024, I was a research intern with the Cancer Data Science Group at the Big Data Summer Institute (BDSI), School of Public Health, University of Michigan. My research interests include—though are not limited to—selective inference, randomization, and spatio-temporal statistics.
